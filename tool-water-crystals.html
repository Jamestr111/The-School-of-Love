In the 1990s, Dr. Masaru Emoto discovered that water exposed to loving words, music, and prayer formed beautiful symmetrical ice crystals. Water exposed to harsh words formed broken, chaotic patterns.

The human body is approximately 70% water. Every session here is an act of saying something beautiful to your water. To yourself.
